No-face

カオナシ Kaonashi, lit. "Faceless"

About

No-Face is a lonely spirit with strange abilities. The greed of the bathhouse transforms No-Face into a monster. Chihiro saves him from the bathhouse's influence and No-Face settles at Zeniba's cottage.

No-face and Chihiro riding the sea train.

No-face's Characteristics

  1. Transmutation He can transform mud into illusory gold.
  2. Omnivore He can eat anything. A kitchen's worth of food, a kitchen's worth of people... a kitchen.
  3. You are what you eat. When he eats a person, No-Face gains their voice and abilities.

No-face's friends
